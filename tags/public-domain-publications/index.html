The Splash of a Drop The Splash of a Drop Images come from a publication from 1897 titled The Splash of a Drop by Professor A. M. Worthington. The publication is essentially lecture notes for a presentation he delivered, and it now exists within the public domain. The original features five pages of illustrations, collectively presenting 30 hand-drawn frames of a drop if mercury. Mouse over or touch for animation.
