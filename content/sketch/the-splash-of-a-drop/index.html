The Splash of a Drop

Images come from a publication from 1897 titled The Splash of a Drop by Professor A. M. Worthington, which was essentially lecture notes, and exists within the public domain. The original features five pages of illustrations, collectively presenting 30 hand-drawn frames of a drop if mercury. Mouse over or touch for animation. Thank you Prof. Worthington!
